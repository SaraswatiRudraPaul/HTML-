VEG FRIDE RICE RECIPE

How to make Veg Fried Rice:

Veg Fried Rice :Veg Fried rice is an easy to make Chinese style meal. There are many varieties for it such as egg fried rice, chicken fried rice and this one here is full of flavourful spices, veggies and the leftover rice that you can saute and prepare in minutes!

Ingredients of Veg Fried Rice:

  1. 2 Tbsp oil

  2. tsp ginger-garlic paste

  3. 1/2 cup spring onion, chopped

  4. 1/2 cup carrot, chopped

  5. 1/2 cup cabbage, chopped

  6. 1 capsicum, chopped

  7. 1 tsp salt

  8. 1 tbsp soy sauce

  9. 1 tbsp vinegar

  10. 1 cup rice

How To Make Veg Fried Rice:

1.Heat oil in a pan.

2.Add ginger garlic paste to it, saute a little.

3.Now add spring onion, carrot, cabbage and capsicum.

4.Saute and cook them well together.

5.Now add salt along with soy sauce and vinegar.

6.Mix thoroughly till simmer and add cooked rice to the pan.

7.Combine the mix with rice thoroughly.

8.Serve your hot Veg Fried Rice in a bowl, garnished with coriander leaves.
